Healthcare Systems

Healthcare decisions must be explainable, reproducible, and clinically defensible.

PROBLEM

Inconsistent eligibility

Same patient data can produce different outcomes.

Opaque decision paths

Clinicians cannot trace how decisions were made.

Audit challenges

Decisions cannot be reliably reproduced later.

WHY CURRENT SYSTEMS FAIL

Healthcare systems rely on mutable workflows, manual overrides, and probabilistic AI — leading to non-deterministic outcomes.

WITH MANTHAN

Deterministic pathways

Identical clinical input produces identical decisions.

Transparent logic

Rules and criteria are explicit and versioned.

Defensible outcomes

Every decision can be explained and validated.

EXAMPLE

Treatment eligibility

input:
  age: 65
  condition: diabetes
  severity: high

rules v1:
  age > 60 AND severity == high → ELIGIBLE

output:
  ELIGIBLE
